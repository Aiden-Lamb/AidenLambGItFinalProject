About my Mom

My Mom's name is Karla Lamb. She's a High School Math teacher at Hampton High School, she teaches from Grade 10 Foundations, all the way up to AP Calculus. She used to be a Math Teacher at Hampton Middle School, but after almost 20 years, she wanted a change of environment, so she applied to be moved up to High School and got the job in 2019.

Theatre has been a big part of everyone in my family's life, my parents both did it as kids, and me, my sister, and Elly did it from Middle School to the end of High School and even now if the opportunity presents itself. So my Mom is very involved with the Theatre Company at Hampton High School. Since she started working there, she has been in charge of doing all of the props for the shows and being in charge of the Stage Crew. Recently however, she has moved up in position to Producer and is now handling all of the Finances and promotion of the shows on top of doing props as well.

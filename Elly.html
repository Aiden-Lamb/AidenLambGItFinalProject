About Elly

This is Elly, she's been my girlfriend for almost a year and a half. We first met in middle school theatre, but really started to hangout 2 years ago. Her favourite things to do in her spare time are to crochet random projects for herself and for her friends, as well as reading. She reads a lot of different books, and it's really fun listening to her talk all about her favourites and the ones she's currently reading. She spends a lot of her time listening to music, which she mostly listens to Taylor Swift. She didn't grow up listening to her music like a lot of other people I know, but she discovered her a few years ago and was instantly obsessed. She wanted to go see Taylor Swift in concert, but unfortunately tickets were almost impossible to get.

Elly also likes to play a few sports. Her two favourites are Rugby and Cross Country, she loves to run so they are a great fit for her. Her favourite will always be Rugby, she first started playing in Grade 9 and has continued to play every year since. This year, she's hoping to be the team captain and for it to be her best year yet. I love going to see her games and seeing her have lots of fun.

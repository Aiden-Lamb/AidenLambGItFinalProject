About me

My name is Aiden Lamb, I'm 18 years old and I was born in Saint John, but I've lived in Hampton my whole life. I graduated from Hampton High School in 2023 and am currently attending NBCC Saint John taking the Information Technology: Programmer Analyst course. I started to get really into coding when I was in grade 12 and I took a Computer Science class. The teacher who taught the class was very knowledgable and although I had no prior knowledge of coding, I caught on really quick and was instantly hooked. I ended up making a working weather station with my teacher as my final project and it's still hooked up on the roof of Hampton High School collecting weather data to this day. Before this class, I had no idea what I wanted to do with my life and wasn't sure of anything post secondary wise, but my parents asked me if I wanted to look into courses at NBCC involving computer science as they could tell I was loving it. The second semester of Grade 12, I took a Cybersecurity course with that same teacher and loved that as well. That's when my parents and I came across the ITPA course. It seemed to align with everything I loved doing already, and the course is a prerequisite for the Cybersecurity course, so it worked out perfectly.

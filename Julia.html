About Julia

Julia is my older sister, she's 20 years old and attends the University of New Brunswick in Saint John studying accounting. She is a very smart and talented person. She was the valedictorian of her graduating class at Hampton High School in 2021, and still continues to have over a 4.0 gpa to this day. That hasn't stopped her from having lots of free time though, she loves spending time with her friends, such as her roommate Sydney and her boyfriend Chris. On weekends, Julia teaches dance to young kids at a dance studio in Hampton that she has attended for almost her entire life.

Julia has a very decorated career in competitive dance. She has attended almost all of the major dance competitions in eastern Canada since she was 5 years old and has consistently made it to the finals and winning medals or trophys. It has gotten to the point where she has many large ziploc bags filled with dance medals, and only the very special trophys get showcased anymore. Her largest accomplishment in dance however, was when she was invited to be a part of Team Canada at the Dance World Cup in Portugal in 2019 and earned a Bronze Medal.
